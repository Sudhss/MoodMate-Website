Conversation Modes

Four voices.
One truth.

Different moments call for different kinds of honesty. Switch modes whenever the conversation demands it.

01 / 04
Sarcastic
Wit with a point. Cutting remarks that still carry care underneath.
"Sure, because ignoring it always works out brilliantly."
02 / 04
Brutally Honest
No softening, no flattery. Just a clear reflection of what is.
"That plan has three obvious failure points. Want to name them?"
03 / 04

Warm, present, non-judgmental. For the days when you just need to be heard.
"That sounds really hard. You're handling more than most people realise."
04 / 04
Neutral
Clean, factual, unfiltered. Pure signal with no emotional overlay.
"Based on what you've described, here are three possible interpretations."

How it
works.

Everything runs on your machine. Nothing leaves your device.

1
You choose the mode

Pick the conversational voice that fits the moment — sarcastic, honest, supportive, or neutral. Switch at any time without losing context.

React frontend
2
Flask routes your message

Your message is sent to the Python Flask backend with a mode-specific system prompt. No external API calls — the request stays entirely local.

Python + Flask
3
LLaMA 3 generates the response

Ollama runs LLaMA 3 directly on your machine. The model produces a response conditioned on the active personality prompt and your conversation history.

LLaMA 3 via Ollama
4
History is stored locally

Every conversation is persisted in a local SQLite database. Browse and revisit past chats from the sidebar — no account, no sync, no cloud.

SQLite
